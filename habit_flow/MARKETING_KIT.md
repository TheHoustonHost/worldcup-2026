## Elevator Pitch

**One sentence:** HabitFlow is the first habit tracker with built-in AI coaching that gives you personalized tips to actually stick with your habits.

**Who it's for:** Adults who want to build positive habits but struggle with motivation and consistency.

## Key Messages

1. **AI-Powered Coaching** — Unlike basic trackers, HabitFlow's AI analyzes your habits and gives personalized advice
2. **Simple but Powerful** — Easy to use, but with features that actually drive behavior change
3. **Works Offline** — Track habits anywhere, anytime
4. **Beautiful Design** — Clean, modern UI that makes tracking satisfying
